Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

1. Eligibility for Refunds

We provide full transaction refunds under the following parameters:

  • Products containing manufacturing defects or dead battery units.
  • Incorrect variant or gadget model delivered.
  • Unused packaging returned within our 30-day trial guideline.

2. Refund Processing Time

Once our sorting centers receive and inspect your returned package, we approve the request and issue a credit refund. The credit is sent to your original credit card or Stripe token. Refunds settle on statements within 5-7 business days.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

We do not issue credit refunds for devices containing clear physical drops damages, custom firmware mods, or water exposures past IP ratings limits.
