Our Mission

Why Dopahr?

"Dopahr" is the Hindi word for the afternoon—the time of day when the sun rides highest in the sky and UV levels surge to their peak. That's when your skin needs protection most. We built Dopahr to deliver weightless, premium, invisible shield formulas that turn daily suncare into a delightful ritual.

The Science of Suncare

Many people think sunscreen is only for hot beach days. In reality, UV rays penetrate clouds and glass daily, causing premature aging, cell damage, and dehydration.

At Dopahr, we focus on broad-spectrum filters. This means our sunscreens shield you from both:

  • UVA Rays (Aging): Long wavelengths that penetrate deep into the dermis, breaking down collagen and causing wrinkles.
  • UVB Rays (Burning): Shorter wavelengths that affect the outer layer of skin, causing sunburns and redness.

Our formulas also include blue-light filters to shield skin from screens (HEV light) and pollution (antioxidants).
