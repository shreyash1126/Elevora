Shipping Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

1. Order Processing Thresholds

Orders are verified, packed, and released to international couriers within 1-2 business days. During peak sale periods, processing may take up to 3 business days.

2. Delivery Methods and Transit Durations

We provide two shipping classes at checkout:

  • Standard Insured Shipping: FREE for all orders above $100 (otherwise flat $9.99). Delivery takes 5-10 business days.
  • Express Courier Service: Flat $9.99. Package departs via priority courier (DHL/FedEx) and arrives in 2-4 business days.

3. Shipment Tracking Codes

Tracking numbers are registered with logistics systems within 24 hours of package departure from our sorting hubs. Enter your code in our "Track Order" portal to view live tracking.
