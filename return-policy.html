Return Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

1. 30-Day trial Guideline

We want you to be fully satisfied with your tech accessory. If you aren't completely happy with your gadget purchase, you can return it within 30 days of arrival for a full refund or exchange. Returning items must be in original condition, including boxes and manuals.

2. Step-by-Step Return Instructions

To start a return, complete these simple steps:

  • Email support@elevora.com with your Order ID and subject "Return Request".
  • Our desk will verify the purchase details and email a prepaid courier return label.
  • Place the gadget back in its box casing, tape the label securely to the external package, and drop it off at the designated courier location.

3. Return Shipping Fees

Elevora provides free return shipping labels for all approved returns containing manufacturing faults. For returns based on a change of mind, a flat return handling fee of $5.99 will be deducted from your final refund credit.
