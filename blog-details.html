Are Hybrid Active Noise Cancellation Headphones Worth It?

Admin Author • July 10, 2026 • 5 Min Read

Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) has quickly become the primary benchmark for wireless audio equipment. However, not all ANC implementations are designed equally. Today, we unpack the engineering of Hybrid systems.

At its core, ANC operates on the principle of phase inversion. Acoustic waves are captured by microphones, processed by an internal DSP (Digital Signal Processor), and played back out of phase—effectively neutralizing environmental sound waves.

Feedforward vs Feedback Configurations

Standard budget systems use feedforward microphones (mounted outside the ear cushion) or feedback designs (monitoring sound directly inside the acoustic chamber).

"Hybrid ANC represents the pinnacle of personal soundproofing, combining both external and internal microphones to block out up to 98% of environmental frequencies."

By positioning microphones both inside and outside the audio driver chamber, headphones like the AeroPulse ANC monitor outside traffic noise while adjusting for leakage or acoustic reflections in real time inside the ear, creating an incredibly quiet auditory space.
