Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you when you complete purchases, register user account details, sign up for news letters, or contact our support desks. This includes details like name, shipping addresses, emails, and transaction history.

2. SSL Payment Gateway Encryption

Your payment security is our top priority. We implement 256-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption protocols. We do not store raw card numbers, CVVs, or bank logins on our server databases. Payments are handled via tokenized API calls through major international card processors (Stripe, Visa, Mastercard).

3. How We Use Cookies

We use local cookies and storage parameters (LocalStorage) to synchronize shopping carts, wishlist preferences, and dark mode choices on your browser. This improves performance and ensures a smooth user experience.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, swap, or trade your personal information with external advertising agencies. We only share details with dropshipping fulfillment centers and international couriers (UPS, FedEx, DHL) to dispatch packages and route shipment tracking updates.
